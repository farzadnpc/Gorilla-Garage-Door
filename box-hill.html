Box Hill has changed dramatically over the past decade. The train station precinct is surrounded by high-rise apartments and new townhouse developments, while the quieter residential streets fanning out through Box Hill North and Box Hill South still carry a mix of original post-war homes and well-renovated Californian bungalows. It's a suburb where the old and new sit side by side — and so do very different garage door systems.

The newer apartment buildings and townhouses often have compact roller doors or aluminium panel doors installed during construction. The older homes on established streets frequently have ageing panel or tilt door systems — some of which haven't been serviced in a decade or more. Springs on these older doors are under constant tension and fail without much warning. We get called to Box Hill regularly for exactly this reason.

Whatever your property type and whatever your door, we have the experience and the parts to fix it same day across all of Box Hill and the surrounding Whitehorse area.
