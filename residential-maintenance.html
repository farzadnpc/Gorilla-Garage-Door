Proactive Servicing

DON'T WAIT FOR IT
TO BREAK DOWN

Most garage door failures are predictable — and preventable. A regular service by a Gorilla technician catches worn parts, misalignment, and tension issues before they become expensive emergency callouts.

Our full residential maintenance service covers every component of your door system. We lubricate, tighten, adjust, test, and report — leaving your door running smoothly and safely.

What's Included

FULL SERVICE
CHECKLIST

🔍

Full Safety Inspection

We check every component — springs, cables, rollers, hinges, tracks, and opener — for wear, damage or safety issues.

🛢️

Lubrication Service

All moving parts are lubricated with the correct lubricant — springs, rollers, hinges, tracks and opener drive system.

⚖️

Balance & Tension Check

We check and adjust spring tension so your door is perfectly balanced — crucial for opener longevity and safe operation.

📐

Track Alignment

Tracks are checked for alignment, levelness and secure mounting. Misaligned tracks are adjusted before they cause bigger problems.

🔩

Hardware Tightening

All bolts, screws, brackets and mounting hardware are checked and tightened. Loose hardware is one of the top causes of door failure.

📋

Service Report

We leave you with a written report of everything we've checked and any items we recommend monitoring or replacing soon.
