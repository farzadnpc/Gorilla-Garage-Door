Panel Issues We Fix

EVERY TYPE
OF PANEL DAMAGE

🚗

Impact Dents

Backed into the door? Minor dents can sometimes be straightened; severe damage means a section replacement — we assess and advise honestly.

💥

Cracked Panels

Steel or aluminium panels that are cracked or buckled need replacing before they compromise the structural integrity of the whole door.

🔩

Hinge & Roller Damage

Bent hinges and cracked rollers cause rough operation and will damage the door further if not replaced. We stock all common sizes.

📐

Bent or Warped Sections

Warped sections cause gaps, draught, and security issues. We replace the affected section and realign the whole door.

🎨

Colour Matching

We carry a wide range of panel finishes and colours. In most cases we can match your existing door so the repair blends right in.

🔧

Bottom Seal Replacement

The rubber seal at the base of the door wears over time. We replace it so your door seals properly against draughts, water and pests.
