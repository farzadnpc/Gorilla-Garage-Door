Repairs We Handle

WE FIX
EVERYTHING

🌀

Broken Springs

Torsion and extension spring replacement — the most common garage door repair. We carry all sizes and replace them safely.

🪢

Snapped Cables

Frayed or broken lift cables repaired and replaced. We retension the system correctly so it's safe and balanced.

⚙️

Opener Faults

Motor not responding, remote not working, door reversing unexpectedly — we diagnose and fix all opener issues.

📐

Off-Track Doors

Door jumped the rails? We realign the tracks, replace bent sections, and restore safe smooth operation.

🔇

Noisy or Slow Doors

Grinding, squeaking, or sluggish operation usually means worn rollers, hinges, or dry tracks. We sort it fast.

🔑

Lock & Handle Repairs

Broken manual locks, bent handles, or damaged emergency releases — replaced and working properly.
