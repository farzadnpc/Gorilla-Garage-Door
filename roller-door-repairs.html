Roller Door Issues We Fix

COMMON ROLLER
DOOR PROBLEMS

🌀

Broken Axle Springs

The spring that drives your roller door is under extreme tension. When it breaks, the door becomes very heavy or won't move at all. We replace these safely.

⚙️

Motor & Drive Faults

Motor not running, remote not responding, or door stopping halfway — we diagnose and repair all automatic roller door motor issues.

📻

Remote & Keypad Issues

Remote programming, keypad replacement, and receiver faults — we get your access controls working reliably again.

🔄

Curtain Damage

Bent, buckled or dented slats in the roller curtain affect operation and security. We straighten or replace damaged sections.

🧱

Guide Rail Issues

Worn, damaged or misaligned guide rails cause the curtain to jam or come off track. We repair and realign the rail system.

🔒

Lock & Security

Broken locks, faulty manual override or worn locking bars replaced so your roller door is secure when closed.
